Check-In Chaos at The Salty Seahorse

**Title: The Unexpected Guests**

*Setting:* The Salty Seahorse, ek quirky, overcrowded bed-and-breakfast jo ek sleepy seaside town mein hai, jahan ke log bade hi eccentric hain aur har saal bizarre festivals hote hain.

---

Maggie Thompson apne bed-and-breakfast, *The Salty Seahorse*, ke entrance pe khadi thi, haath mein ek clipboard tha aur aankhon mein determination. Aaj ka din bada important tha—famous food critic Mr. Kapoor aa rahe the, jinki ek achhi review se unka business ya toh boom karega, ya phir khatam.

“Okay Maggie, aaj sab kuch perfect hona chahiye,” usne khud se kaha. “No more Pajama Pancake Party ya Sock Puppet Sunday. Bas ek simple, classy breakfast.”

Tabhi uske phone pe ek message aaya. “Uncle Jerry aa gaya hai,” Lily ne text kiya, jiska tone pura sarcastic tha. Maggie ne thoda sigh kiya. Uncle Jerry, jo ki Maggie ka distant cousin tha, hamesha apne ajeebo gareeb habits ke saath aata tha aur chaos laata tha.

---

*Scene 1: Uncle Jerry’s Arrival*

Uncle Jerry, ek chhote se suitcase ke saath, porch par khada tha, ek hat pe jisme chidiya baithi thi (haan, sach mein). “Maggie beta! Tumne chai banayi? Maine suna hai yahan ki chai toh world famous hai!” usne excited hokar kaha.

Maggie ne nervously smile kiya, “Bas, Uncle, thoda busy hoon. Critic aa rahe hain.”

Jerry ne apni quirky smile di aur phir ghar ke andar ghus gaya, bina kisi ko notice kiye.

---

*Scene 2: Bob the Neighbor’s “Help”*

Tabhi Bob, jo ki ek overly helpful but clueless neighbor tha, apne haath mein ek ladder leke aaya.

“Hi Maggie! Maine suna hai guest aa rahe hain, toh socha thoda help karoon. Ladder le aaya hoon, roof pe thoda painting karni thi.”

Maggie ne thoda panic mein kaha, “Bob, abhi nahi, please! Critic aa rahe hain!”

Lily, jo apne phone mein busy thi, ne bola, “Bob, tumhari help matlab disaster, yaar.”

Bob ne confused look diya, phir ladder ko porch pe rakh kar chala gaya.

---

*Scene 3: Fluffy’s Antics*

Fluffy, family ka chhota sa dog, jo hamesha kuch na kuch gadbad karta tha, abhi tak chup tha. Lekin jaise hi Uncle Jerry ne apni chidiya wali hat nikali, Fluffy ne socha ki yeh koi naya khel hai.

Wo chidiya ke taraf bhaaga, chidiya udi aur Fluffy ne Uncle Jerry ke slippers chura liye. Jerry ne jhat se slippers dhundhna shuru kiya, aur Fluffy ne porch pe daud lagayi, slippers ke peeche.

---

*Scene 4: Lost Delivery Driver*

Tabhi door pe ek delivery driver aaya, jo confuse tha ki yeh koi surprise party hai. Usne apne haath mein ek bada cake liya tha.

“Excuse me, yeh surprise party kaha hai? Main toh delivery karne aaya hoon,” usne Maggie se poocha.

Maggie ne hairani se kaha, “Surprise party? Nahi, yeh toh ek food critic ki visit hai.”

Driver ne haath se cake ko thoda sambhala, “Acha, toh main yahan galat aa gaya hoon?”

Lily ne phone se video record karna shuru kar diya, “Yeh toh comedy ka perfect scene hai!”

---

*Scene 5: The Food Critic’s Arrival*

Finally, Mr. Kapoor aaye. Maggie ne apne best smile ke saath unka swagat kiya. Lekin jaise hi wo andar gaye, Fluffy ne unke shoe pe chhota sa puddle kar diya, Jerry ne apni chidiya ko critic ke upar chhoda, aur Bob ne galti se ladder gira di, jo ek shelf ko todte hue breakfast table tak aaya.

Mr. Kapoor ne thoda shock mein kaha, “Yeh... unique experience hai.”

Maggie ne socha, “Bas, yeh din bhi toh yaadgar ban gaya.”

---

*Ending:*

Raat ko, Maggie, Lily, Uncle Jerry, Bob, aur Mr. Kapoor sab porch pe baithe the, Fluffy unke beech mein tha, sab hans rahe the.

Mr. Kapoor ne kaha, “Aapka B&B sirf food ke liye nahi, yahan ki vibe ke liye bhi famous hoga.”

Lily ne cheekh kar kaha, “See, mama, chaos bhi kabhi kabhi best recipe hoti hai!”

Sab hans pade, aur Maggie ne socha, shayad thoda chaos hi zindagi ka asli flavor hai.

---

**The End**
